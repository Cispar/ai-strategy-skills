# Opportunity Scoring

Use this skill when the user asks about:

- evaluating business opportunities
- deciding between projects
- investment decisions
- new market opportunities

## Workflow

Score opportunities from 1–10 across:

### Market Size
How large is the market?

### Growth
Is the sector expanding?

### Competition
How crowded is the market?

### Barriers to Entry
How difficult is entry?

### Strategic Fit
Does the opportunity match the user's capabilities?

## Final Score
Calculate a weighted score and explain the result.
