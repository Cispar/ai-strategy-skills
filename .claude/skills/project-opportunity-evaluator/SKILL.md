# Project Opportunity Evaluator

Use this skill when evaluating:

- new projects
- client opportunities
- sector expansion
- partnerships
- investments

## Workflow

### 1 Opportunity Summary
Summarise the opportunity clearly.

Include:
- project type
- client
- location
- approximate scale

### 2 Strategic Fit
Evaluate alignment with:

- current capabilities
- strategic sectors
- long-term growth

### 3 Commercial Potential
Assess:

- potential fee value
- margin potential
- repeat work potential

### 4 Delivery Risk
Evaluate risks such as:

- complexity
- programme pressure
- client reliability
- resource requirements

### 5 Competitive Position
Assess:

- existing relationships
- likelihood of winning
- differentiation

### 6 Opportunity Score

Score from **1-10** across:

- Strategic fit
- Commercial value
- Win probability
- Delivery risk (inverse)

Calculate a final score.

### 7 Recommendation

Provide one of:

- **Pursue aggressively**
- **Pursue selectively**
- **Do not pursue**

Explain reasoning.
