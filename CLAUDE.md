# Parmarbrook Strategy System

Purpose:
Strategic analysis toolkit for evaluating markets, sectors, projects, and business decisions.

Skill pipeline:

1. context-primer
2. market-analysis
3. competitive-analysis
4. project-opportunity-evaluator
5. strategy-operating-system

Execution logic:

context-primer captures company context and constraints.

market-analysis evaluates industry structure and growth drivers.

competitive-analysis evaluates competitors and positioning.

project-opportunity-evaluator scores the opportunity.

strategy-operating-system synthesizes all outputs into a final recommendation.

All skills must finish with:

Recommendation
